Privacy Policy

Battle Of Blackjack · Effective date: July 21, 2026 · Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Battle Of Blackjack (“the App”) handles information. The App is published for the App Store and may show ads via Google AdMob.

1. Who we are

Developer / publisher contact: sbmuratcinar@gmail.com

iOS Bundle ID: com.battleofblackjack.app

2. Information we collect

The App is designed to minimize personal data collection.

  • Gameplay data stored on device: local settings, match progress, and scores needed to run the game on your device.
  • Advertising & measurement (Google AdMob): when ads are allowed under applicable consent rules, Google may process advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location derived from IP, and interaction data about ads, according to Google’s policies.
  • Consent / privacy choices: in regions that require it (for example under GDPR/UMP), the App may present Google’s User Messaging Platform so you can manage ad personalization choices.

We do not require account creation, and we do not intentionally collect names, email addresses, photos, contacts, or precise GPS location inside the App.

3. How information is used

  • To operate core gameplay on your device.
  • To show interstitial ads during a match under the App’s ad rules.
  • To measure ad delivery and combat fraud/abuse as provided by the ad SDK.
  • To honor privacy/consent choices where required.

4. Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Ads may appear as full-screen interstitial ads. Ad serving and related processing are performed by Google. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob & privacy.

Domain-level app-ads.txt for this publisher is published at https://muratcinar10.github.io/app-ads.txt.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Advertising-related data may be processed by Google and its advertising partners when ads are shown. Device OS providers (Apple) may also process data according to their system privacy controls.

6. Retention

Local gameplay settings remain on your device until you delete the App or clear app data. Advertising data retention is controlled by Google according to its policies.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your choices

  • Use in-app privacy/consent options where offered by Google UMP.
  • Use Apple’s tracking / privacy system settings on your device.
  • Contact us to ask questions about this policy.

9. Contact

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
